NOBEL BALL

FEBRUARY 7 2020 AT HOTEL DU VIN, 20:00-01:00


We are very happy to announce our second (alcohol-including) event of the year, the annual Nobel Ball. This is a night where we gather in dresses and black tie to celebrate and remember the great Alfred Nobel, the Swedish inventor of dynamite and more importanly, peace. So clear your schedule and prepare yourself for an explosive amount of Scandos and wine! Guests will be served a three-course meal accompanied by a bottle of wine per head and a prosecco reception. Along with a delicious dinner and abundant amount of wine, guests can expect phenomenally humorous speeches, and a bloody good time.

Ticket price includes:

  • Transport to and from the venue;
  • 3 hours of the best boxing in St Andrews;
  • Afterparty with live DJ.
