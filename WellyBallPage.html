WELLY BALL 2019


NOVEMBER 9 AT KINKELL BYRE, 18:00-02:00


Join us for the 12th annual WELLY BALL on the 9th November 2019! Welly Ball is an unforgettable event, attended by over 2000 students from all over the UK. The evening features a sit-down black-tie dinner and an after-party filled with dancing and DJs. Once again, we are pleased to support the Charlie Waller Memorial Trust - a charity which raises awareness about depression in young people and reduces associated stigmas. For more information on their fantastic work please visit: www.cwmt.org.uk
