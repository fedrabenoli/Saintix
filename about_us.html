ABOUT US

Have you ever missed out on an event because you couldn't get a ticket? We know the feeling - there is nothing worse than watching your friends enjoy themselves over social media and hearing about what a good time they had for days after. That's where SainTix comes in. Designed to help you find the best priced ticket to the event you want to go to, you can browse all up coming events with direct links to the Facebook page of the person selling.
