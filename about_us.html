ABOUT US


The University of St Andrews Boxing Club, with the support of our wonderful sponsor JAR Capital, is delighted to invite you to our 2019 charity home show. Our fighters have been training tirelessly and will be competing at Kinkell Byre in aid of YoungMinds, one of the UK's leading charities supporting young people with mental health issues. So, join us for a drink and watch the product of your fellow students do us proud in the ring.

Ticket price includes:

  • Transport to and from the venue;
  • 3 hours of the best boxing in St Andrews;
  • Afterparty with live DJ.
