THE MERMAIDS CHRISTMAS BALL 2019


DECEMBER 1 AT KINKELL BYRE, 20:00-02:00


Grab your passports and boarding passes as Mermaids invites you to celebrate Christmas Around the World. Join us on the 1st of December for an evening of festive joy and magic! This event is brought to you by Mermaids: The University of St Andrews Performing Arts Fund, a sub-committee of the Union that helps all students in their theatrical endeavours! The funds raised through this event allow Mermaids to provide and facilitate productions and other opportunities in the performing arts.
